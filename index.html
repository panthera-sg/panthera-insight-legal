Legal & privacy

This site hosts the public legal documents for the Panthera Insight mobile app. The app is published by Panthera International Asia Capital Pte. Ltd., an entity regulated under a Singapore Monetary Authority (MAS) licence.

About the app

Panthera Insight is a read-only portfolio reporting application provided to existing clients of Panthera International Asia Capital Pte. Ltd. The app does not process payments, hold funds, or transmit money. Reports are produced by Panthera's portfolio operations team and made available to the named client through the app.

This app provides information only and does not constitute financial advice or a recommendation to invest. Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results. Investments can fall as well as rise.
